My offering

I will help you indetify and achieve your goals

I am an action and goal oriented person coming from an international business environment with an IT background. These characteristics are reflected in my coaching approach, where I place great emphasis on the correct perception of reality and the identification and achievement of goals.

What will you get?
As a coach, I will:
  • Help you explore the reality in a broader context.
  • Look behind your beliefs and consider all the perspectives that are beyond your perception.
  • Identify the real goal you want to achieve.
  • Help you plan the process of change in concrete steps and find ideas on how to achieve your goals.
What do I offer?
I offer coaching in an online format, where face-to-face meetings are replaced by video calls.

I provide coaching in two languages - Czech, English.
Who is this type of coaching suitable for?
Online coaching is primarily used by people for whom time flexibility and less time commitment are important. At the same time, thanks to the online format, it is possible to arrange early morning and evening sessions.

How do I perceive coaching?

Benefits of coaching

Life is defined by the choices you make and the paths you choose to follow. While some exceptional individuals may have an innate ability to navigate life's decisions with confidence, most of us face internal barriers and beliefs that limit us. I see coaching as a journey where a coach serves as a guide, helping people achieve their goals by unlocking their hidden potential, seeing beyond those barriers, and transforming their beliefs to align with reality. In my experience, the two things people often need most are an increased awareness (perception of reality) and clarity on their true goals and desires. Once these are in place, everything else tends to fall into place naturally.

Your journey is just beginning. Remember, the sky is the limit!

Increase your self-awareness

Coaching helps you discover your values, goals, strengths and areas that need improvement. In this way, you can better understand your motivations, allowing you to make more informed decisions.

Achieve your goals

A coach will help you create clearly defined goals and set specific steps to achieve them.

Overcome obstacles

A personal coach supports you in overcoming internal and external obstacles that prevent you from achieving your goals.

Increase your life satisfaction

Personal coaching helps you improve your lifestyle and achieve a balance between work, family, interests and personal development.

Coaching adapted to today's hectic world

Why choose online coaching?

More comfort

Coaching at a time that suits you and from the comfort of your own home or other favourite location.

Availability from anywhere

You can join from all over the world, which means you are no longer limited to physical meetings. You'll save time and money on travel.

Efficient use of time

Utilizing an online format allows you to schedule sessions to fit your unique schedule.

Lower price

Online coaching eliminates the costs associated with travel that are reflected in the price of physical meetings.

About me
I am currently in the process of obtaining my Associate Certified Coach (ACC) certification from the International Coaching Federation (ICF), of which I am a member. My main training for the role of a coach is the Business Coaching Diploma Intense course, which is accredited by the ICF. In addition to coaching, I work primarily as a manager in an international consulting company where I lead a team of 11 people who specialise in providing cyber security services.
